### üåå Long-Term Vision (Next 12 Months and Beyond)

Our long-term vision for Open Micro SaaS is to provide a comprehensive toolkit for building SaaS applications that are scalable, secure, and user-friendly. Here are some of the features we're considering for the future:

- **[Internationalization Support](https://github.com/product-makers-hub/open-micro-saas/issues/1)**: Making it easier to create multi-lingual applications to reach a global audience.
